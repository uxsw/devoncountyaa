Welcome to Devon County Athletic Association the umbrella organisation for Devon County athletic clubs. Devon County AA promote County championships in Track and Field and Cross Country, we also organise an Indoor Sports Hall league for young athletes and the Track and Field Devon Open series for all age groups 8 years old and upwards.

Southern Athletic League
There is an opportunity for athletes in the U17/U20/Seniors and Masters age group to take part in the Southern Athletic meetings this year. The first one is on Sunday June 20th at Exeter. There will be a cost of £6 to take part and places will be limited. The athletes can use their own implement weights and hurdles heights. The programme is attached. There is a link to a Google form below where athletes can apply to take part. Confirmation of a place will be sent through a few days before. Athletes from COPAC, Erme Valley, Tavistock, Exeter, Newquay & Par and Cornwall do no need to use this form but should get in touch with their team managers.
