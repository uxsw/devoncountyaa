Offical website for Devon County Athletics Association

Welcome to Devon County Athletic Association the umbrella organisation for Devon County athletic clubs. Devon County AA promote County championships in Track and Field and Cross Country, we also organise an Indoor Sports Hall league for young athletes and the Track and Field Devon Open series for all age groups 8 years old and upwards.

Read our latest news

We support road running and Fell running events such as Devon's oldest road event the Ross Shield team race, currently held within Tiverton Harriers Bampton - Tiverton Easter Monday race. Further information on all these events under the listed headings

CLUB ACTION POINTS:

The next Committee Meeting is on 20th September. There will be no meeting in August. Remember any affiliated club that wishes to send a representative to this meeting can do so.

Remind your club members to enter the South West Track and Field Championships at Exeter Arena on 20th August. Athletes can enter on line at http://swic.athletics-uk.org Entry fees £8 first event, £5 each further event. Closing date for entries Sunday 6th August.

County Teams team managers will be sent a list of athletes who have entered from which to make the County team selection.

The next Devon Open Series competition is on Sunday 13th August at Braunton. Encourage your Club members to take part.
