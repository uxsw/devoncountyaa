All registrations will take place on the day - no pre-entry this year.

And, just to be absolutely clear, this is an open event so is not restricted to anyone who is 8 years old or more. So, if you are not a member of an athletics club or, perhaps, you are on holiday in the area, you are equally as welcome to participate.

This year we are giving a medal to all who take part in all 4 meetings.

The entry fee will be £4 for juniors and £5 for seniors.
