INFORMATION FOR ATHLETES AND TEAM MANAGERS:

ANYONE WHO WANTS TO BE CONSIDERED FOR THE DEVON TEAM MUST ENTER THE CHAMPIONSHIP

CLUB VESTS MUST BE WORN AND NUMBERS MUST BE WORN AS ISSUED

Numbers must be securely attached to the vest by 4 pins so as to be clearly visible. If only one number is issued it must be worn on the FRONT of the vest.

Medals will be awarded to the first team in each race. Senior Men 6 to count, all other categories 3 to count. The first 6 finishers for a Club in the Senior/Vet Men's Race and the first 3 finishers for a Club in the Senior/Vet Women's Race will score as the Senior Team. The first 3 Masters to finish will score as the masters A team, the next 3 as B team etc even if they score in the Senior team. Medals will be awarded to the 1st, 2nd and 3rd individuals and to the first 3 teams in each race. (Masters individual medals in each 5 year age group) DUE TO COVID RESTRICTIONS NO MEDALS WILL BE PRESENTED ON THE DAY. Results will be published on the evening of the event. No results on the day. Medals will be forwarded to clubs when possible.

Parking is available at Exeter Arena. Please observe guidelines on social distancing.

Race information and numbers will be available from a tent near the Exeter Harriers Clubhouse. Toilets are available in the clubhouse but on a strictly controlled basis. Face coverings are required to enter building. No changing available. There will also be portable toilets available.

The course is undulating grassland. First Aid will be provided.

THE START AREA WILL BE COVID SECURE AND ONLY REGISTERED COMPETITORS AND OFFICIALS WILL BE ALLOWED IN THIS AREA. SENIOR ATHLETES SHOULD NOT BRING ANY SPECTATORS WITH THEM AND JUNIOR (UNDER 18) ATHLETES CAN BRING ONE ACCOMPANYING PERSON WITH THEM WHO MUST BE REGISTERED ON THE SYSTEM. There are public areas near the course but we will be requesting that any members of the public avoid close proximity to the course.

Entry fee £5 Juniors (U13-U17) and £7 Seniors (includes U20 and Masters). All entrants must have a County qualification by birth or residence.
