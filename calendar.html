These are the full instructions and terms and conditions applicable when entering.

Events and Race Times (there will be wave starts of max 30 runners. First wave will go off at times shown and then at 2 min intervals.)

Please note: ANYONE WHO WANTS TO BE CONSIDERED FOR THE DEVON TEAM AT THE INTER COUNTIES MUST ENTER THE CHAMPIONSHIP.

Parking is available at Exeter Arena. Please observe guidelines on social distancing.

Access to this area will be signposted. PLEASE USE THE PATH NEXT TO THE RAILWAY LINE DO NOT CROSS THE COURSE OR ANY RUGBY PITCHES

SENIOR ATHLETES SHOULD NOT BRING ANY SPECTATORS WITH THEM AND JUNIOR (UNDER 18) ATHLETES CAN BRING ONE ACCOMPANYING PERSON WITH THEM WHO MUST BE REGISTERED ON THE SYSTEM. FULL RISK ASSESSMENT IS AVAILABLE HERE >>>

Limited toilets are available in the clubhouse but on a strictly controlled basis. Face coverings are required to enter building. No changing available. There will be portable toilets available near the start.

The course is undulating grassland. First Aid will be provided.

Race information and numbers will be available from a tent near the Start area in Eastern Fields. This will be a COVID secure area where only registered athletes, supporters and officials will be able to enter.

CLUB VESTS MUST BE WORN AND NUMBERS MUST BE WORN AS ISSUED
Numbers must be securely attached to the vest by 4 pins (please bring your own) so as to be clearly visible and worn on the FRONT of the vest.

Medals will be awarded to the first team in each race. Senior Men 6 to count, all other categories 3 to count. The first 6 finishers for a Club in the Senior/Vet Men's Race and the first 3 finishers for a Club in the Senior/Vet Women's Race will score as the Senior Team. The first 3 Masters to finish will score as the masters A team, the next 3 as B team etc. even if they score in the Senior team. Medals will be awarded to the 1st, 2nd and 3rd individuals and to the first 3 teams in each race. (Masters individual medals in each 5 year age group) DUE TO COVID RESTRICTIONS NO MEDALS WILL BE PRESENTED ON THE DAY. Results will be published on the evening of the event. No results on the day. Medals will be forwarded to clubs when possible. THE START AND FINISH AREAS WILL BE COVID SECURE AND ONLY REGISTERED COMPETITORS AND OFFICIALS WILL BE ALLOWED IN THIS AREA. There are public areas near the course but we will be requesting that any members of the public avoid close proximity to the course. If you are not a registered supporter then you will also have to follow those rules.

RESULTS WILL BE POSTED ON THE ENTRY WEBSITE AND ON THE DEVON COUNTY AA WEBSITE

Please be aware that you must abide by the following Terms and Conditions at the time of entry and on the day of the event. If you live in Tier 3 area please do not attend the event in line with Government advice.
