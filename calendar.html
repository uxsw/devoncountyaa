After finishing runners up to Wiltshire last year, Devon will be entering teams again this year.

1. Glastonbury Round the Tor 10k, Sunday 30th April.
2. The Great West Run (half marathon) Sunday 15th October (date TBC)

Runners wishing to be considered for selection or clubs wishing to nominate runners for selection are invited to phone or text team manager, Dave Phillips - 07868 494168. Teams of 6 men and 6 women for both events.
